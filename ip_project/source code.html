Ramagya Sports Academy is always on the lookout for latent talent, spots them & provides them training irrespective of age. Potentially talented sports persons are enrolled in free training programmes undertaken by our Academy where the best of opportunities, international infrastructural facilities, regular updates on performance & great mentors hone the skills of these budding talents and make them prospective medal winners at the world stage.
